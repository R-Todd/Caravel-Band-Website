About the Band

Caravel is a four-piece rock band based in Flagstaff, Arizona, formed in 2024. We write and perform original music rooted in classic rock, 90s grunge and modern alternative rock. Known for their energetic live performances, Caravel quickly rose in the local scene after winning the Flagstaff DUSK Battle of the Bands. Their debut album 'World on Fire' is out now!
